PHILOSOPHY


The Philippine Integrated School (PIS) Foundation Inc. in pursuant to its mission is committed to the development of young ones in the social responsibility and contribution towards the transformation of the society, to attain deeper understanding in faith in the commitment to the continuing pursuit of truth, knowledge, moral and ethical values in their chosen field and to be the center in the search of truth and excellence.

MISSION


To equip student/pupil in the higher form of academic excellence, to be a home for achievers and competitor, to supply quality manpower required for the progress of the society that is relevant and accessible to all and to promote harmonious living between Muslim and non-Muslim, and to cater quality customer reality.

VISION


The Philippine Integrated School (PIS) Foundation Inc. aims to be the lead of excellence in moulding the young ones to be academically competitive, to be the institution providing high quality standards of education, to be with the rapid change of technology, and to prepare students/pupil to be globally competitve, morally upright and socially sensitive. It seeks to promote understanding of life, religion and reality.

OBJECTIVES


The objectives of the school are the following:

1. To promote and advance relevant quality education in the field of language humanities, mathematics, natural sciences, social sciences, vocational educational, and information technology.

2. To provide scholarship grant for the fortunate but deserving students.

3. To promote programs that are morally beneficial that will alleviate economic problem of Filipino people.

4. To purchase, recieve, acquire, take, grant, hold, sell, lease, pledge, exchange, mortgage, and otherwise deal in real and personal property as transaction of its lawful affairs and are necessary or useful to carry out objectives and purposes of this institution.

5. To organize, establish, and operate projects or any form of enterprise connected with the growth and development of the school, which may directly on indirectly its goals and objectives.
